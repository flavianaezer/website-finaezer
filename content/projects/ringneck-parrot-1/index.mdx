#### Acrylverf op Papier, 2021, Eigen collectie
Veel papegaaien wonen in onze buurt en komen graag langs. Ze herinnen me aan de papegaaien in Mumbai.

    
English: Their screeching sounds fill the skies, scare the guinea pigs, and remind me of my childhood where they lived in the mango tree opposite our kitchen window. These green birds spend most days in the neighbor's willow tree.
