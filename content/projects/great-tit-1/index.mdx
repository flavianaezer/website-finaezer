#### Acrylverf op Papier, 2021, Eigen collectie
Koolmees van de tuin, ze zijn er veel te vinden en komen graag beurten.


English: Great tits ('Koolmees' in Dutch) are found here in plenty. Along with the blue tits, they frequent the bird feeder, and are often found on our garden fence. We hope that one of these will nest in our bird house.
